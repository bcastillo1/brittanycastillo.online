Photography

Mirror into time

Photography, for me, is a mirror into a past time or moment. It accepts the reality of then to showcase to the now. I have been honored to take pictures for family, friends, and strangers. I love taking portraits and trying to capture the unique personality of the individual. The way their eyes squish a little bit when they laugh, or how they express their love to their spouse.

I also had the opportunity to do some product photography for the EWU tech department. It was a first and unique experience for me. I learned how to capture the essence of an object. There were a variety of objects ranging from bottles to records, barbwire to arrow heads. Through my weeks of work, I discovered I actually really enjoyed the still life photos I was taking. That I wasn’t just photographing a bunch of random items to display digitally, I was photographing history. As I said, it was a unique experience. One I would love to repeat again.

If you find yourself in need for a photographer, feel free to give me a shout! Contact me on Instagram or Twitter, or you could email me at design.brittany@outlook.com
